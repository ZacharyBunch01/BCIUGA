BCI Research Organization

Advancing brain-computer interface research and education at the University of Georgia.

Purpose

The BCI Research Organization was founded to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and research in the field of BCI (brain-computer interface) at UGA. We connect undergraduate and graduate students with opportunities in neurotechnology, data science, engineering, and cognitive science.

Current Goals

  • Analyzing open-source BCI/EEG datasets
  • Composing white papers and research proposals to support future BCI initiatives at UGA
